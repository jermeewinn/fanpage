Meet Jeff Montalvo, a.k.a. Seven Lions

Who is Seven Lions?

Jeff Montalvo, aka Seven Lions, is a professional DJ from Santa Barbara, CA. He is affiliated with Ophelia Records, and specializes in genres of EDM such as melodic dubstep, trance, and electro house, among others. A full collection of his music dating back to 2010 can be found here.
